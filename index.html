Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

Scrambled eggs with mashed avocado on whole grain toast. Nutritious and delicious breakfast choice for a great start!

Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them until well mixed.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the beaten eggs.
  3. Stir the eggs gently with a spatula until they are cooked through but still moist.
  4. While the eggs are cooking, toast the slices of whole grain bread until golden brown.
  5. Mash the ripe avocado in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the eggs are cooked, remove them from the skillet and place them on top of the toasted whole grain bread.
  7. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the scrambled eggs.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
Blueberry Pancakes

Fluffy pancakes filled with juicy blueberries and drizzled with maple syrup. A classic breakfast favorite that's sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges look set, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  8. Serve the pancakes hot with maple syrup drizzled on top.
Greek Yogurt Parfait

Indulge in layers of creamy Greek yogurt, crunchy granola, and fresh berries—a refreshing and protein-packed breakfast choice to savor.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Granola
  • Fresh berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by layering a few spoonfuls of Greek yogurt into the bottom of a glass or bowl.
  2. Sprinkle a layer of granola on top of the yogurt.
  3. Add a layer of fresh berries.
  4. Repeat the layers until the glass or bowl is filled, finishing with a final layer of berries on top.
  5. Serve immediately as a refreshing and protein-packed breakfast option.
Vegetable Omelette

Fluffy omelette filled with sautéed vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and spinach. A healthy and satisfying breakfast choice.

Vegetable Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, spinach)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil or butter for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Chop the assorted vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little olive oil or butter.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables to the skillet and sauté until they are tender.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs until well mixed and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour the beaten eggs over the sautéed vegetables in the skillet.
  6. Allow the eggs to cook without stirring until they are set around the edges.
  7. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to let the uncooked eggs flow underneath.
  8. Once the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute or until the eggs are cooked through.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot as a healthy and satisfying breakfast choice.
Caprese Salad

Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves drizzled with balsamic glaze. A light and refreshing salad that's perfect for lunch.

Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into evenly sized slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato slices on a serving platter.
  3. Place a slice of mozzarella cheese on top of each tomato slice.
  4. Tear the fresh basil leaves and sprinkle them over the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  5. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing salad option.
Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas

Nutritious quinoa tossed with chickpeas, cucumbers, tomatoes, and a lemon vinaigrette dressing. A satisfying and protein-rich salad option.

Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa
  • Chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • Cucumbers, diced
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Lemon vinaigrette dressing (made with lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper)
  • Optional: chopped fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, chickpeas, diced cucumbers, and diced tomatoes.
  3. Pour the lemon vinaigrette dressing over the salad and toss until well combined.
  4. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro, if desired.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature as a satisfying and protein-rich salad option.
Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Whole grain wrap filled with sliced turkey breast, creamy avocado, lettuce, and tomato. A wholesome and portable lunch option.

Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain wrap or tortilla
  • Sliced turkey breast
  • Ripe avocado, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato, sliced

Instructions:

  1. Lay the whole grain wrap or tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Arrange the sliced turkey breast, avocado slices, lettuce leaves, and tomato slices in the center of the wrap.
  3. Fold in the sides of the wrap and then roll it up tightly from the bottom.
  4. Slice the wrap in half diagonally, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately as a wholesome and portable lunch option.
Vegetable Stir-Fry

Colorful medley of stir-fried vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas served over brown rice. A flavorful and nutritious lunch dish.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • Brown rice, cooked
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Sesame oil
  • Optional: sliced tofu or chicken for added protein

Instructions:

  1. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat and add a little sesame oil.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the assorted vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. If using tofu or chicken, add it to the skillet and cook until heated through.
  5. Season with soy sauce to taste and toss to combine.
  6. Serve the stir-fried vegetables over cooked brown rice.
  7. Enjoy immediately as a flavorful and nutritious lunch dish.
Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Creamy Alfredo sauce tossed with tender chicken breast and fettuccine pasta. A comforting and indulgent dinner choice for pasta lovers.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast, cooked and diced
  • Alfredo sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the Alfredo sauce over medium heat until warmed through.
  3. Add the diced chicken breast to the skillet and stir until heated.
  4. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the Alfredo sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the chicken Alfredo pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
